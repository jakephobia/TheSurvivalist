Tools

What each tool does

Edger — EDGIC editor. Build rating charts (UTR/MOR/CP/OTT + tone + visibility) and export as image.

Outlist — Seasons ranking. Rate past seasons by cast, editing, twists, winner, location and more. Custom weights.

Simoa — Season simulator. Run a full simulated season with tribes, swaps, events, tribal councils and alliances.

Smuffer — Season progress tracker. Track tribes, advantages, alliances, EDGIC, confessionals, power rankings and tribal votes episode by episode.

Sutral — Winner prediction algorithm. Enter cast and episode data to get win probability estimates.

Tally — Fantasy draft game. Draft castaways, track points from immunity wins, eliminations, idols and other events.

Torcha — Gacha card game. Collect Survivor contestant cards, open packs and battle with your favorites!
