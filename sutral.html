What is SUTRAL?

SUTRAL is an algorithm that tracks the winning potential of remaining Survivor players, episode by episode, taking into account parameters both internal and external to the game.

It has also proven useful in predicting eliminations: more than 3 out of 4 times, the next person eliminated is in the bottom 3 according to SUTRAL's calculations.

How does the score calculation work?

The score is based on:

  • Alliances: in the Alliances column, enter the number of alliances explicitly shown by the edit for each player. Check Majority if one of the alliances is the majority in the tribe;
  • Confessionals: enter the number of confessionals the edit gave to each player (recommended: @survivorfactchecker on IG);
  • EDGIC: enter the Edgic tag applicable to the player in the episode, without the visibility number;
  • Idols and Advantages: enter the number of idols/advantages/beware advantages each player has per episode.

The tool calculates a score per episode and returns rankings and power rankings. It also shows each player's overall path throughout the season.

How to enter data?

  1. In Episode 1, add names with the + button or use the batch import at the top of the page;
  2. Enter all parameters (journey, eliminated, medevac);
  3. Click Calculate All at the bottom: the tool computes scores and win equity;
  4. Click Add Episode to move to the next episode with remaining players.
